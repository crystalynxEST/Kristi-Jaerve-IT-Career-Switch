How to Use the Map:

1. Country Selection: Choose a county by clicking on the country select drop-down menu.

2. Exploring Locations: Double click/tap anywhere on the map to select that country and receive it's information.


Right Side Buttons:

  • Magnifying Glass Icon: Search for any location on the map by clicking on the icon, then enter its address, postcode, or name.
  • Layers Icon:
    • Adjust the map layout by selecting different visual options.
    • Modify the markers displayed in the selected country area by toggling the desired markers.

Left Side Buttons:

  • Zoom In/Out: Use the "+" and "-" buttons to zoom in or out.
  • Globe Icon: Explore the demographics of the selected country.
  • News Button: Access available news for the country. (The button is hidden if no news is available.)
  • "W" Icon: Retrieve Wikipedia information about the selected country.
  • Arrows Button: View currency exchange information.
  • Sun Icon: Access detailed weather information.
